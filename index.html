Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?
For bridal appointments, we recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance, especially during wedding season. For other events, 1-2 months in advance is usually sufficient.
How long does mehendi last?
Our natural henna typically lasts 1-3 weeks depending on your skin type, aftercare, and exposure to water. The color will deepen in the first 48 hours and gradually fade over time.
Do you travel for appointments?
Yes, we provide mobile services throughout Lucknow and surrounding areas. Travel fees may apply depending on location.
What safety measures do you take?
We use only natural, chemical-free henna cones. All application tools are sterilized and we follow strict hygiene protocols. We also provide aftercare instructions to ensure the best results.
