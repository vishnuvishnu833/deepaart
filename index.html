My Journey

I began my journey as a mehendi artist over 12 years ago, learning traditional designs from my grandmother in Lucknow. What started as a passion soon became my profession as I mastered various styles including Rajasthani, Arabic, and modern fusion designs.

My journey has been filled with memorable moments - from designing mehendi for brides on their most special day to creating intricate patterns for festivals and celebrations. I believe that mehendi is more than just body art; it's a cultural tradition that connects generations and brings joy to celebrations.
