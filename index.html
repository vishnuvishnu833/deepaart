Frequently Asked Questions

How many days before should I book?
It's recommended to book at least 1-2 months in advance for weddings to ensure availability. For other events, 2-3 weeks notice is usually sufficient.
Do you take Mehendi bookings outside Lucknow?
Yes, we provide services throughout Lucknow. Travel charges may apply for locations outside Lucknow city limits.
Is the advance payment refundable?
No, the advance is not refundable as it locks your date and time and confirms your booking.
How long does the application take?
Bridal designs typically take 3-5 hours depending on complexity. Guest designs usually take 30-90 minutes per person.
